About Simple Tools

A simple and privacy-friendly collection of free online tools designed for everyday tasks.

Who We Are

Simple Tools is a free online platform created to help users complete everyday tasks quickly and efficiently. All tools work directly in the browser and require no installation or registration.

What We Do

We provide a collection of lightweight online utilities such as word counters, BMI calculators, timers, generators, and converters. Each tool is built with simplicity, accuracy, and usability in mind.

Our Purpose

Our goal is to make useful tools freely accessible to everyone. We believe that common tasks should not require complex software, paid subscriptions, or unnecessary data collection.

Who Can Use Simple Tools

Simple Tools is suitable for students, writers, developers, designers, office workers, and anyone who needs quick and reliable tools for daily use.

Transparency & Trust

We respect user privacy and transparency. Simple Tools does not require user accounts and does not store personal data. All tools run locally in your browser.
