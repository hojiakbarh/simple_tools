Free Body Mass Index Calculator

The BMI Calculator helps you calculate your Body Mass Index using your height and weight. BMI is a commonly used indicator to classify underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity.

To calculate your BMI, enter your weight in kilograms and your height in centimeters, then click the calculate button. The result is shown instantly.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or higher

This BMI calculator works directly in your browser and does not store any personal data. It is suitable for quick health-related checks.
