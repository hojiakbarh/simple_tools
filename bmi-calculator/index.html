Free Body Mass Index Calculator

The BMI Calculator by OnHub helps you calculate your Body Mass Index using your height and weight. BMI is a widely used indicator to categorize body weight and assess potential health risks.

Enter your weight in kilograms and height in centimeters, then click the calculate button to get your BMI instantly. This tool works directly in your browser without storing any personal data.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI below 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or higher
