Free Online Text Case Converter

This free text case converter allows you to change text to uppercase, lowercase, sentence case, or capitalized words instantly. It is useful for writers, students, programmers, and content creators.

Simply paste your text into the box above and choose the desired case format. The conversion happens instantly in your browser without any registration.

Available Case Options

  • Uppercase – convert all letters to uppercase
  • Lowercase – convert all letters to lowercase
  • Sentence case – capitalize the first letter of each sentence
  • Capitalize words – capitalize the first letter of every word

This tool works entirely client-side and does not store or send your text anywhere.
